Houston Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name is chosen as "Houston Chamber of Commerce" to represent the city of Houston and its business community. The word "Chamber" signifies the affiliation with the chamber of commerce, an organization dedicated to promoting economic growth and development within the city.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Houston Chamber of Commerce website is to serve as a hub for business networking, economic development, and community engagement. It aims to provide valuable resources, support services, and information to local businesses and entrepreneurs. Additionally, the website will facilitate connections between businesses, promote local events, and advocate for policies that foster a thriving business environment in Houston.

Scenarios

  • What upcoming events are organized by the chamber to promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What is the current economic outlook for Houston, including population growth and industry trends?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the website will primarily consist of:

  • Main Heading Color: #0066CC
  • Accent Color: #FF9933
  • Background Color: #F4F4F4
  • Text Color: #333333

Typography

The following fonts will be used throughout the website:

  • Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif
  • Body Font: Roboto, sans-serif
